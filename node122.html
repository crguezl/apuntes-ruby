283 is not a commonly used statement. In practice, it is much more common to redefine a method with a new 284 statement than it is to undefine or delete the method.

Note that the 285 statement must be followed by a single identifier that specifies the method name. It cannot be used to undefine a singleton method in the way that 286 can be used to define such a method.
